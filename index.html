Overview

Build guilds is Hack Club's
1-day hardware meetup!

Running April 13th-19th in cities all around the world. Every guild is organized by a local Hack Clubber in which Hack Club funds orgs per signup!

Who?

Anyone can attend, no hardware experience needed. However you must be of 13 to 18 years of age.

Where?

Community spaces, makerspaces, schools, the organizer picks a safe, hardware-friendly venue.

What?

Workshops, project demos, lightning talks, soldering sessions, and open build time. Every guild will have different events!

How Much?

Completely free to attend. Hack Club funds per attendee to cover food, venue, and materials.

Global Week

100+ guilds running globally the same week. Same energy, same excitement, just different area.
