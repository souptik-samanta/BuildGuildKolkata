Schedule

What's the plan?

A fast-paced flow for the day. This schedule is subject to change during the event.

10:00 AM

Kickoff & Intro

Doors open, registration, and a quick briefing on the day's build goals.

10:30 AM

Electronics Basics (Speedrun)

A rapid-fire introduction to components and circuits to get everyone building.

11:15 AM

Build Phase 1: The Foundation

Start your project. Focus on getting the core functionality working on your breadboard.

12:30 PM

Lunch Break

Quick recharge and networking with fellow builders.

01:00 PM

Build Phase 2: PCB & Customization

Design your PCB in EasyEDA and add creative features to your project.

02:30 PM

Final Polish & Debugging

Last-minute fixes and making sure everything is ready for the showcase.

03:15 PM

Project Showcase & Photos

Demo your work to the group and grab a few team photos.

03:45 PM

Closing & Wrap-up

Final notes, resource sharing, and pack-up. Event ends at 4:00 PM.

What is Hack Club and Build Guild?

Hack Club

Hack Club is a global nonprofit community for teenage makers. It helps students learn by building real projects and running local events.

Build Guild

Build Guild is Hack Club Blueprint's 1-day hardware meetup format. Local organizers host hands-on sessions where teens can build, learn, and share projects together.
